About me
I'm a postdoc (Juan de la Cierva Fellow) in the Number Theory group at ICMAT, Madrid. Short CV
  • 2023 – 25: Postdoc (EPSCR Doctoral Prize Fellow) @ UCL
  • 2022 – 23: Postdoc @ King's College London
  • 2018 – 22: PhD @ LSGNT (with David Burns @ King's College London)
  • 2016 – 18: M Sc @ LMU Munich


My primary mathematical interests are:
  • Special values of \(L\)-functions, particularly the Tamagawa Number Conjecture of Bloch and Kato and its equivariant refinement (the `eTNC').
  • Stark's conjecture and its integral refinements.
  • Euler systems
  • Iwasawa theory
